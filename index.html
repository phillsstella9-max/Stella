FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be traditionally published to work with you?

No. I work with self-published, hybrid, and traditionally published authors. My strategies work for all publishing paths. Whether you're just starting or have been publishing for years, I can help you reach the next level.

What if I'm a first-time author?

Perfect. Many of my clients are debut authors. I'll guide you step-by-step so you don't make the expensive mistakes most first-timers make. You'll launch your first book with the strategy of a seasoned pro.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see measurable results within 30-90 days. Bestseller status can happen in as little as 48 hours with the right launch strategy. The timeline depends on where you're starting and how quickly you implement.

What genres do you work with?

All genres. Fiction, non-fiction, memoirs, business books, self-help, romance, thrillers, children's books—I've done them all. The fundamentals of book marketing and positioning work across every category.

What if I don't have a big budget for marketing?

Many of my strategies don't require paid ads. I'll show you organic tactics that work even on a shoestring budget. Smart positioning and strategic launches often outperform big ad budgets anyway.

How do I know if we're a good fit?

Apply to work with me. I review every application personally and only work with authors I know I can help. If we're not the right fit, I'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction.
